Frequently asked questions

What is SchengenAppointments.com and how does it help me?

Booking a Schengen Visa appointment in the UK, USA, Ireland, ... is a painful process! You have to constantly monitor multiple VFS websites and telegram groups, or pay hefty fees to travel agents.

So to save you time and money, this website is the fastest way to track all available visa appointments in your city in a single, easy-to-read table. To make the most of it:

  • - Regularly visit this website to check latest appointment availabilities.
  • - Utilize the 'Pick City' dropdown menu at the top to select your desired city.
  • - Remember to pick between the 'Tourism' and 'Business' visa types using the tabs.
  • - Set up instant email alerts to be notified of new appointment openings!
What countries and visa centers are currently supported?

Presently support Tourism and Business visa appointments for the following VFS visa centres:

From London, Birmingham, Cardiff, Manchester, Edinburgh: Austria, Croatia, Cyprus, Czechia, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, Hungary, Iceland, Italy, Latvia, Lithuania, Malta, Netherlands, Norway, Portugal, Slovenia.

From Dublin: Austria, Croatia, Denmark, Finland, Iceland, Netherlands, Norway, Sweden.

From New York, SF, LA, Houston, Chicago, Boston, Washington, Atlanta, Miami : Austria, Croatia, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Iceland, Latvia, Lithuania, Netherlands, Norway, Portugal, Slovenia, Sweden

From Toronto, Vancouver, Ottawa, Montreal, Edmonton : Austria, Croatia, Denmark, Estonia, France, Lithuania, Netherlands, Norway, Slovenia, Sweden

From Dubai, Abu Dhabi : Austria, Croatia, Czechia, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Greece, Hungary, Italy, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Netherlands, Norway, Portugal, Sweden, Switzerland, Cyprus, Latvia

More destinations are on the way! If you'd like to see your city supported, please email feedback@schengenappointments.com

How often is the appointment data updated?

To ensure you see the most up-to-date information, the data is pulled from visa center websites as frequently as possible - usually every few minutes, with a maximum delay of 15 minutes. You will see a 'checked X mins ago' timestamp next to each appointment. If you see an appointment listed here but not on the official VFS website, it likely means someone else already booked it.

Does this service automatically book appointments for me?

No, it's fraudalent to buy appointments from any intermediary, and the visa centres strongly condemn this practice. To protect your privacy and ensure a legitimate application, it's always best to book your own appointments directly.

We built this to be a free, fair and privacy preserving service for everyone. It charges an optional small fee to cover the server costs.
